Coach Nate is currently a Brown Belt under Prof. Jt and has been training for almost 8 years. Originally from California, Coach Nate fell in love with Jiu Jitsu while serving 5 years in the army. Eventually, Nate went on to teach combatives within the army but, after serving his time in the service he knew that he wanted to pursue jiu jitsu as a career. Nate moved back home to California where he started to study under the Mendes Bros and begin to teach jiu jitsu at Art of Jiu Jitsu. After some time receiving his purple belt from the Mendes Bros, he decided he needed a change of pace from the West Coast and moved to NY to study under Prof. JT.
