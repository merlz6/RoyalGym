Alicia Lutes

2020

Alicia is the owner and a children’s instructor at Royal Jiu Jitsu. She grew up in Albertson, New York, but was always in Mineola with her friends. She is a Blue belt under Marcelo Garcia. Alicia is also a teacher as well as a mental health professional. She believes in physical activity being crucial to a person’s spiritual, mental, and physical well being. She focuses on competition training and continuing to become a better Jiu Jitsu instructor for children.

Athos Miranda

2020

Athos is a black belt in Brazilian Jiu jitsu. He started training BJJ 15 years ago in Belo Horizonte, Brazil. Athos was training under Ricardo Luiz, one of the biggest names in his city. He had bigger goals and decided to leave his home to chase them. Athos made it to NYC to live out his dream training under Marcelo Garcia. As a result of training at Marcelo’s academy he has achieved the greatest results in his career, including his NOGI Panamerican title. Athos and Matheus have been training together for years; making each other’s Jiu jitsu grow. Athos is super excited to be part of Royal Jiu Jitsu, bringing with him high quality jiu jitsu and friendships
